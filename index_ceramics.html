Quetzalcoatl Vessel

Centering around the Aztec serpent God Quetzalcoatl, this vessels represents my cultural background and remembering those who came before us.

Coffee cup #1

Constructed from the coiling method, this ceramics cup was painted with a high-fire glaze so the person who has this cup in their possession can use it on a daily basis.

Coffee Cup #2

Contructed using the slab method, this ceramics cup, is one of the largest in the series and the only one with an added texture element.

Coffee Cup #3

Contructed using the slab method, this cup is the only in the series that was left with unglazed portions.

Coffee Cup #4

The final cup in the series this cup serves more as a creamer cup as well as a cup to froth milk.
