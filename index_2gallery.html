Photography

Practicing in this field for eight years, photography has been my primary focus of art and my focus through college. Over the course of time, my style has developed into street and urban landscape photography.

Photoshop

Coming from a background in photography, photoshop was an inevitable process to learn. Though photography and photoshop connect through post-processing, photoshop has taken on a different genre then what I naturally do. Photoshop has been a way for me to explore surrealist art.

Illustrator

Working on illustrator for a little over a year, illustrator has become a platform for me to create artwork that has a minimalist as well as an abstract approach. Along side my artwork, illustrator has also become a way for me to create artwork for logos.

Glitch Art

Only recently learning about glitch art, this genre of art ahs become a way to create fun pieces that can allow me to create bodies of work simply for my own pleasure.

Video

While only working with video and after effects for a short amount of time, video and after effects has become a second way for me to create street style work that has always been done through photography.

Ceramics

Creating works in this field for two years, ceramics has become a way for me to create conceptual in a three dimensional way.
