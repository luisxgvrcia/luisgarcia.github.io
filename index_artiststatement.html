My work, often centering around a minimalist style, has become something that I have come to develop over the course of six years. The bodies of work that I generally tend to make stick around the world of photography. Though recently, has been expanded into other fields such as ceramics, video, painting, and linoleum prints. Typically, my work tends to center around the idea of it just looking "nice", however as of recent, has taken a more conceptual approach. My more recent work has taken ideas and meanings that have been incorporated into them.
